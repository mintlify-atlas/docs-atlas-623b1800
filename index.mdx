## Key Features

Justina combines cutting-edge simulation technology with AI-powered analysis to provide comprehensive surgical training and validation.

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Surgical Training & Education">
    Provide surgeons with a safe, repeatable environment to practice complex procedures and improve their skills without risk to patients.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Robot Design Validation">
    Test and validate surgical robot designs in simulation before committing to expensive physical prototypes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Performance Assessment">
